La Posada

The finest tapas dining experience serving authentic Iberian cuisine in the heart of Brighton!


La Posada is a newly opened restaurant in the heart of Brighton, founded by the highly experienced and passionate chef, José Marquez Blazquez. With his extensive culinary expertise and love for Spanish cuisine, Chef José aims to bring the enchanting flavors and vibrant atmosphere from his hometown of Sevilla to your table here at La Posada.

The menu at La Posada showcases a wide range of classic Spanish tapas dishes, each bursting with exquisite flavors and made from the freshest traditional ingredients. From age-old favorites such as patatas bravas and gambas al ajillo to lesser-known regional specialties, every plate pays homage to the diverse culinary heritage of Spain.

To complement the delectable cuisine, Chef José has meticulously designed an inviting ambiance. The restaurant's décor features rustic elements, evoking the charming atmosphere of an authentic Spanish tavern, while contemporary touches provide a stylish, modern twist.
